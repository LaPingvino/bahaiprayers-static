*“Blessed is the Spot” in the Sliammon Language

Oh kw shet hegus

Poleset toh nehs kweh eh sheet hega tah ah yehs, nehs klumes, nehs tah kluk kweh nahs, hega tah t’akt, hega tah tapas, hega tah sapet, hega tah geejah, hega tah cheh, hega tah kwuthys, kw ty ty nigit nons kw shet hegus. Yeech kw cheh hum. Hega tah nisheeyeok, helh awh nehs.

(Source category: Tla’amin Nation) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 6854)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
