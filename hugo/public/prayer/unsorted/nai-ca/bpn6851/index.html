*“Blessed is the Spot” in the language of the Gwich’in First Nations.

BAHA’I EENJIT GIGIINJIK

Gwiizrii gwijuu’ee gwahtsii, ts’at jii nakhwahzheh, ts’at jii diikaiik’it, ts’at jii diidrii, ts’at ddhah, ts’at galchiijaa, ts’at nin an, ts’at antl’at, ts’at nanh, ts’at chuu choo ts’at njuu, K’agwaadhat nijin nanh chyah gwaltsaii, ts’at yinji’gwichil’ee ts’at ch’agwahdit.

(Source category: Gwich’in) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 6851)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
