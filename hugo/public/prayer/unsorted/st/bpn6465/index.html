Prayer bpn6465 in Sesotho

<< Sesotho

Ho tlotlisoe Lebitso La Hau, Oho Molimo Oa ka! Ke Uena Eo lintho tsohle li U rapelang Le Ea sa rapeleng letho, Eo E leng monghali oa lintho tsohle le Eo e seng mohlanka le hoa e le ‘ngoe ea tsona. Ea tsebang lintho tsohle le E oho seng letho le Mo tsebang. U ratile ho itsebisa batho; ‘me la lebaka leo, U hlahisitse tlholehong tsohle tse teng ka lentsoe la molomo oa Hau; ha ho Molimo o mong kantle ho Uena, Moqapi, E Moholo, le Ea Matla ka ho fetesisa. Kea U rapela, ka baka la lona lentsoe len le khantseng holim’a ho chaba ha thato ea Hau, ho nthusa ho noa ka botebo metsi a phelang ao ka oona U hloekisitseng bakhethoa ba Hau ‘me Ua tsosa meea ea bohle ba U ratang,e le hore le ‘na ka nako tsohle le boemong bo fe le bo fe, ke tle ke retelehetse sefahleho s aka ho Uena. Ke Uena Molimo Ea Matla, Oa Khanya le Mohau. Ha ho Molimo o mong haese Uena feela, ‘Musi Oa tsohle, Ea Khanyang, Ea Tsebang tsohle.

(Source category: Poko le teboho) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 6465)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
