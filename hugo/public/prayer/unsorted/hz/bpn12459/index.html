Prayer bpn12459 in Namibia

<< Namibia

Nafimanekwe, Kalunga, omo lwo vatumwa vaye vo hole Ovo hetu tu mine! Ove ou uli omwenyo nouyelele wetu, yukifa ovapiya voYe mendyila doYe, ndele tu pa oupuna tu ku kalele auke. Kalunga, tu honga ouliumwe woYe, opo tu hatale umwe ehefi Ove. Ove omunefilonghenda no muyandyi womilongelo doYe do Kupa Onghenda! Kalunga, shitila omundilo wohole momitima do vapiya voYe; wo uxuikemo omadila-dilo mai ndele ove auke ukale muvo. Kalunga, tu likila oukwalushe woYe ou waliko nale no taukala ko alushe fiyo alushe, osheshe Kapena Kalunga ehefi ove. Ove auke Kalunga. Oshili, mwove ohatu mono enghono nehekeleko ta li di kwoVe. Bahá̓u̓lláh

(Source category: Oshikwanyama : Omailikano Amwe) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 12459)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
