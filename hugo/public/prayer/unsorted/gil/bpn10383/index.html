Prayer bpn10383 in Kiribati

<< Kiribati

First line: O Thou Whose face is the object of my adoration O Ngkoe Are Moamoam bon boton au tangira, Are Tamaroam bon au nono, Are mwengam au kouru, Are kamoamoaakim bon au kantaninga, Are Am kakaawakin bon raaraou, Are Am tangira bon bukin karikau, Are atongan Aram bon karauakiu, Are Kaanim nakoiu bon au kantaninga, Are Menam I rarikiu bon nanou ae e korakora ao au kantaninga ae te kabanea n rietaata; I bubuti Nakoim bwa Ko na aki tukii mai irou bwaai ake Ko katauii ibukiia naake a rineaki ibuakoia Am toro. Kataubobongaai, ngkanne, ma tamaroan te aonnaaba aio ao ae e na roko. Ngkoe, ni koaua, bon Ueaia aomata nako. Akea riki te Atua bwa ti Ngkoe, te Kabanea n Nanoanga, te Kabanea n Tituaraoi.

(Source category: Te Buoka ao te Ibuobuoki) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 10383)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
