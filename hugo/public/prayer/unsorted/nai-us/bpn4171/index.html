Prayer bpn4171 in American Indian

<< American Indian

Shil beehozin, Diyin Shitaa’ Nínílíni, éí nééhodeessiilgo biniighé iiná shaa dííní’ á áádóó Niji’ sodiizin do. T’áá k’ad bee haszíí’ shich’i’ nahwii’ná áádóó Ni t’éiyá Nidziil, té’é’í shidaah ndahkai áádóó Ni t’áá altsoní Niholó. T’áá Ni t’éiyá Aláadi Diyin nilí, Nihich’i’ Nahwiilná’ígíí T’áá línínsin, Hool’áágóó Hooniló.

*I bear witness, O my God, that Thou hast created me to know Thee and to worship Thee. I testify, at this moment, to my powerlessness and to Thy might, to my poverty and to Thy wealth. There is none other God but Thee, the Help in Peril, the Self-Subsisting.

(Source category: Navajo) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 4171)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
