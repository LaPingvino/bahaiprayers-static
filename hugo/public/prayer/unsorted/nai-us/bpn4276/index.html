<< American Indian

DA-MEH UPEH, DA-MEH UPEH, SEMES DA-NEH-MEH-NEH HA BEYUH MEH, DEAS MA-DE-ZA-POING DA BE-YAH NA-HA-NEE-DOH SOO- WI-KH DA-MEH UPEH DE-NEE TA GWAP DEAS DA DE-NEE-TA-GWAP KOOPH NAHK. MA DE DE-MA-ZI, UPEH, SU-KA MA-DEH HA-NEE DOH, DEAS GU-TA MA DEH M-EH SOO-NEEK SEE-DEH U-MA-BEE-ZA-KHON-DOH. DAMEH UPEH. GA DEAS MA-DEH ZA-PEH-TINDH, MA-DEH-DAH-TI-GEEK, MA-DEH NA-SOO BA-DU KHAN DOH, SOO-KA DA-NA-BOO-IPH. DEAS MA-DEH BE-YUH ZA-NEE-SHOON-GAH, MA-DEH GA-ME-NGH.

*O my God! O my God! Unite the hearts of Thy servants, and reveal to them Thy great purpose. May they follow Thy commandments and abide in Thy law. Help them, O God, in their endeavour, and grant them strength to serve Thee. O God! Leave them not to themselves, but guide their steps by the light of Thy knowledge, and cheer their hearts by Thy love. Verily, Thou art their helper and their Lord.

(Source category: Shoshone) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 4276)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
