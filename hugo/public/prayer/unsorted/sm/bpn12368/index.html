Prayer bpn12368 in Samoan

<< Samoan

O God! Educate these children. Le Atua e! A’oa’oina nei tamaiti. O nei tamaiti ua o ni laau o Lau faatoaga, o fugalaau o Lou fanua laugatasi, o rosa o Lou togalaau. Ia toto ifo i o latou luga Lau uaga, ma ia susulu ifo i o latou luga le La o le Upu Moni faatasi ma Lou alofa. Ia fanaufouina i latou e Au matagi malu e mafai ai ona a’oa’oina, ola, ma atiina’e, ma faaalia i le matagofie silisili i latou. O Lau Afio o le Foa’i, O Lau Afio o le Mutimutialofa.

(Source category: CHILDREN) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 12368)

-- Abdu'l-Bahá
