O fy Arglwydd, rwyf wedi ymddiried fy ysbryd a’m holl fodolaeth i ddeheulaw Dy nerth a Dy amddiffyn, a rhoddaf fy mhen ar fy ngobennydd trwy dy rym,a’i godi yn ôl Dy ewyllys a Dy fodd daionus Di. Tydi, yn wir,yw’r Amddiffynnwr, y Ceidwad,yr Hollalluog, y Mwyaf Grymus.

I have committed, O my Lord, my spirit and my entire being into the right hand of Thy might and Thy protection, and I lay my head on my pillow through Thy power, and lift it up according to Thy will and Thy good-pleasure. Thou art, in truth, the Preserver, the Keeper, the Almighty, the Most Powerful

(Source category: Evening) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 13335)

-- Bahá'u'lláh
