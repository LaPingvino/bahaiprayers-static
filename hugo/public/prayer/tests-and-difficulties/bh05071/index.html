Prayer BH05071 in Afrikaans

Geprys en verheerlik word U, o my God! Ek smeek U by die sugtinge van U liefhebbers en by die trane deur diegene vergiet wat verlang om U te sien, om nie U tere genade van my in U Dag weg te hou nie, nòg my die melodieë te ontneem van die Duif wat U eenheid loof voor die lig wat van U aangesig skyn. Ek is die een wat in ellende is, o God!

Prayer BH05071 in English

Lauded and glorified art Thou, O my God! I entreat Thee by the sighing of Thy lovers and by the tears shed by them that long to behold Thee, not to withhold from me Thy tender mercies in Thy Day, nor to deprive me of the melodies of the Dove that extolleth Thy oneness before the light that shineth from Thy face. I am the one who is in misery, O God!
