Prayer BB00002ENA in English

O Lord! Enable all the peoples of the earth to gain admittance into the Paradise of Thy Faith, so that no created being may remain beyond the bounds of Thy good-pleasure. From time immemorial Thou hast been potent to do what pleaseth Thee and transcendent above whatsoever Thou desirest. (Source category: Triumph of the Cause) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 351)

Prayer BB00002ENA in Esperanto

De pratempoj Vi estas potenca fari kio ajn plaĉas al Vi kaj transcendanta ĉion ajn, kion Vi deziras. (Source category: Triumfo de la Afero) (Bahaiprayers.net ID: 4358)
