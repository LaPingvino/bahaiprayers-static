Prayer bpn6216 in Luganda

##ESSALA EY’ETTEEKA ENYIMPI Essala ey’etteeka enyimpimpi esomwa omulundi gumu buli lunaku wakati we tuntu nenjuba ngegwa. Mpa obulizi, Ayi Katonda wange, nti Gwe wantonda okuKumanya nâ€™okukusinza. Nkakasiza ddala, mu kaseera kano, obutesobola bwange era no buyinza Bwo, obwavu bwange ku buggaga Bwo. Tewali Katonda mulala wabula Ggwe, Omuyambi mu kabi, Eyemaririra. *“Essala ez’etteeka ezisomwa buli lunaku, ziri satu… Omukkiriza wa ddembe okulondawo nga bwayagala, emu ku satu, naye alagirwa mu teeka, okusoma emu kuzo, era nga atukiriza obulombolombo bwayo ugigenderako”.
