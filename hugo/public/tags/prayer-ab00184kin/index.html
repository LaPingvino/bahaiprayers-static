Prayer AB00184KIN in English

##Revealed to the Bahá’ís of the Central States *Let the spreaders of the fragrances of God recite this prayer every morning: O Lord, my God! Praise and thanksgiving be unto Thee for Thou hast guided me to the highway of the kingdom, suffered me to walk in this straight and far-stretching path, illumined my eye by beholding the splendors of Thy light, inclined my ear to the melodies of the birds of holiness from the kingdom of mysteries and attracted my heart with Thy love among the righteous.
