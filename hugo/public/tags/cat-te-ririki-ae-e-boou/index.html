Prayer bpn10466 in Kiribati

*(Naw-Ruz, Maati 20 ke 21, bon moan bongin te ririki ni Bahaâ€™i.) Bon Am bwai te kamoamoaaki, Ngkoe Atuau, bwa Ko a tia ni katabua Naw-Ruz bwa te tai ni kukurei irouia naake a tia ni mwannanoa te Aki Mamatam i bukin Tangiram, ma ni kaawakiniia man baike aki raraoi Iroum. Au Uea anganiia ain Am tangira, ao te kabuebue are e otinako man te Aki Mamatam are Ko anga, bwa a na konaa ni urarake iai i nanon Am Waaki ao karikiia bwa a na maeka ma Kamoamoaam ma Uringam.
