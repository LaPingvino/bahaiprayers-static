Wat aimagam uruga at outu koewa, at pa, amba pa sewa, amba at bumaggewa, amba sunup, amba at darak, amba at dagup, amba at bumak, amba at piup, amba puiman, amba at guguwa, amba pon ap gapan Maman yauwa wane, amba Me ganaseden wat taragasen.
