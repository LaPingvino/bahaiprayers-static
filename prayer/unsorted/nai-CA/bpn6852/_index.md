*(Na  Ité ithui, ethui ninghinta sothan eyínjyái Hutsha-dun, itsua, iney, imiua, ijya:
* Blessed is the Spot


Ninghinta Sothan Jaül

Ninghinta sothan jaül,
 na ku, na jaül, na kashee,
Na etsi, 
na dhawe, 
so-aji, nund ninachi,
Na unclout, 
na nünd, 
mun sa'cho, 
chui, 
tl'ok caüa
Jáüa úzhi Ilé ilsi, 
Ilé uhuaná sulhan.

(Source category: Northern Tutchone)
