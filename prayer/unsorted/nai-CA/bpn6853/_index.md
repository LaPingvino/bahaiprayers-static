*The prayer "Blessed is the Spot"  in Stó:lõ Shxweli Halq'eméylem, the language of the Soowahlie People.


Le plist te shxweli      
Qas te lalem             
Qas te shxweli           
Qas te tówel            
Qas te th'ale           
Qas te smelmalt         
Qas shxwelis eyolemthét 
Qas te siyótkõwel       
Qas te spelhqél         
Qas te téméxw           
Qas te kw'otl'kwa       
Qas te tl'chá:s         
Qas te spelhqél         

Shxwelis kwes qwelesem te chichelk Siyam shxwelis seys kwes thelhílem

(Source category: Stolo Shxweli Halqemeylem)
