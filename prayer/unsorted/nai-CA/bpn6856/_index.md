*"O God, Guide Me" in Sm'algyax, the language of the Tsimshian Peoples.

O Miyaani               
Da'a'ntgi               
Lusndaamti              
sü gugweelksm laawksi   
ada so goy'p'm biyaalsi.and  
Nüün gu daanmsgit        
di£ na gatgyedn
