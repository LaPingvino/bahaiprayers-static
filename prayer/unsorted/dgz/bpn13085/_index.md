Nop ma nainit wamewa gapan waen ge apan mu dere, waenapan dere den wagup namumup yona wain, ma yogup kanamawa gwanap wan.  (Kitáb-i-Aqdas)

“Nu uruga, Ipiwa gapan Maman iup mega ae amat tait.”
