#NGURANGURA
	##(MORNING)

	Nina pinduka mouvando Woge, Ove Karunga gwange, ano yamuwapera ogu a papara uvando, a kare monzugo zoge zoKupongoka megameno Lyoge nouvando welipopero Lyoge. Twedera monda zange, Hompa gwange, nosite sokupuma-kwezuva sehoramo, ngwendi moomu ono twedere nge site songurangura soufenkenda Woge.
